THE SPICE OF LIFE

“This year I’m basically trying to capture the essence of the perfect ‘cloud 9’ moment. It’s elevated from ordinary life, but it’s also fleeting, so it’s that much more important to appreciate it while it’s here. That’s how I’ve felt about my birthday the past few years - getting to bring all my favorite people together and curate an experience for them. So I figured, why not make that the theme?

Here’s what to expect:

  • Al Fresco: There’s a sizzle in the air during a Chicago summer - we’re going to tap into that. Expect to be in environments where we can bask in the sunshine, feel the breeze, and let loose.
  • Rejuvenating: Though I expect this weekend to be a lot of fun, it won’t be at a live-fast-die-young, burned too bright, breakneck pace. It’s a time to treat yourself to an indulgent, but laid back weekend.
  • Soulful: During the weekend’s schedule, I hope we find pockets of depth too! Reconnecting with old or new friends and sharing moments that remind me how lucky I am to have friends like you!”
